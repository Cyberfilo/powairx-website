What is STEM Racing?

STEM Racing is a school project where students use science, technology, engineering, and mathematics to design and build small, high-performance racing cars.

Each team works like a real racing organization, combining engineering, testing, and presentation skills.

Our Team

Our team is international, ambitious, and driven by engineering and creativity. Despite being one of the youngest teams, we compete at a professional level.
