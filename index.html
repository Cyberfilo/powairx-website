What is STEM Racing?

STEM Racing is an international STEM competition where students design, build, and race miniature Formula 1 cars in a fast-paced, real-world learning environment. The challenge brings together participants from over 60 countries globally and pushes students to combine engineering, innovation, and creativity with marketing and teamwork. Through the process, competitors develop practical technical skills, problem-solving abilities, communication, leadership, and strategic thinking, gaining hands-on experience that mirrors how real motorsport and engineering teams operate in the professional world.

Each team works like a real racing organization, combining engineering, testing, and presentation skills.
